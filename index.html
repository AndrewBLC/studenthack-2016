About

StudentHack is a the original student hackathon in Manchester, where students come together, team up and collaborate to build cool stuff. Turns out we are run by students, too.

We have always focused on #learn #build #share module, and this year we are aiming to make it more accessible than ever. By working together with Major League Hacking and GreatUniHack team to bring an awesome inclusive weekend to all who are tech enthusiastic!

StudentHack IV


StudentHack IV is back. On 4-6th March 2016, with the same epicness with more inclusively!

StudentHack will start at 5pm Friday and runs overnight until 5pm Sunday. Judging will be done in a science fair style, with top 10 chosen teams presenting in front of everyone for 3 minutes each, followed by 2 minutes Q&A. Top 3 winners will be chosen by our panel of judges. Each sponsor get to choose the winner of their own challenge.

Visit the tickets section for info on how to register for StudentHack IV.

Tickets FAQ

Are tickets free?

Yes. They are 100% free!

Who can get a ticket?

Enrolled students or students who graduated in the last 12 months, with passion for technology (mandatory).

What if I study outside the UK?

You are still eligible. If you are outside Europe, you may need to apply for a visa. We can't sponsor visas.

I am under 18 can I still get a ticket?

Absolutely, if you are over 14 years old you can get a ticket and please fill in these forms, print them and bring them with you on the day Consent form, Photography policy, Medical record

FAQ


I haven't coded much. Should I come?

Hackathons are for beginners. Networking with and seeing awesome hackers in action will expose you to a tremendous amount of knowledge. Amazing suit of industry experts and mentors that are happy to assist you!

What should I bring?

Bring your laptop, phone and chargers. Other things to consider: spare clothes, adapters, hardware and a sleeping bag, oh and don't forget your enthusiasm! We are going to provide you with all food, swag, sleeping rooms and drinks you will need to keep you going.
